# 1. Introduction

Welcome to EasyApps ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our Discord bot services, including our Normal Bot and White-Label Bot services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described herein.

# 2. Information We Collect

# 2.1 Information Collected from All Users

# 2.1.1 Discord Server Information

When you add our bot to your Discord server, we may collect:

  • Server ID
  • Server name (for operational purposes)
  • Channel IDs where the bot is active

# 2.1.2 Configuration Data

When you run /server_setup or configure the bot, we collect and store:

  • Server ID
  • Configuration settings you provide
  • Channel assignments
  • Role configurations
  • Any custom settings you specify

# 2.1.3 Application Data

When you create and use application features, we collect:

  • Server ID
  • Log channel ID
  • Application name and configuration
  • Application templates and questions

When an application is submitted, we store:

  • Server ID
  • User ID (of the applicant)
  • Message ID
  • Application responses

# 2.1.4 Operational Logs

We collect operational logs including:

  • Command usage logs
  • Error logs and debugging information
  • Bot activity logs
  • Performance metrics
  • Timestamps of bot interactions

These logs may include partial message content necessary for command execution, troubleshooting, and service improvement.

# 2.1.5 Message Access

Our bots require access to Discord messages in servers where they are deployed to function properly. This includes:

  • Reading messages to detect and execute commands
  • Processing message content for bot functionality
  • Monitoring channels where the bot is active

Important: We do not store full message content except as necessary for command logs, error logs, and operational purposes.

# 2.2 Additional Information from White-Label Customers

# 2.2.1 Account Information

When you create a White-Label account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Account settings and preferences

# 2.2.2 Payment Information

When you subscribe to White-Label services, payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your complete payment card information. Stripe may collect:

  • Payment card details
  • Billing address
  • Transaction history

Please refer to Stripe's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle your payment data.

# 2.2.3 Bot Token Information

For White-Label customers:

  • We generate and securely store Discord bot tokens
  • Tokens are accessible only to authorized EasyApps management personnel
  • Token security is maintained through encrypted storage

# 2.2.4 Customization Data

We store white-label customization information including:

  • Bot name
  • Bot avatar/logo
  • Branding preferences
  • Visual customization settings

# 3. How We Use Your Information

# 3.1 Service Operation and Delivery

We use collected information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Execute bot commands and features
  • Process application submissions and workflows
  • Provide bot functionality in your Discord servers
  • Manage white-label bot instances

# 3.2 Technical Support and Troubleshooting

We use collected information to:

  • Diagnose and resolve technical issues
  • Respond to support requests
  • Debug errors and improve bot performance
  • Investigate service disruptions

# 3.3 Service Improvement and Analytics

We use collected information to:

  • Analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Improve bot features and functionality
  • Develop new features
  • Optimize performance and reliability
  • Understand how users interact with the Service

# 3.4 Communication (White-Label Customers Only)

We may use your email address to:

  • Send important service updates and announcements
  • Notify you of Terms of Service or Privacy Policy changes
  • Respond to support inquiries
  • Send billing and subscription information
  • Provide technical assistance

We do not send marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in to receive them.

# 3.5 Security and Compliance

We use collected information to:

  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • Ensure compliance with Discord's Terms of Service
  • Protect the security and integrity of the Service
  • Respond to legal requests and enforce our rights

# 4. Data Storage and Retention

# 4.1 Data Storage Location

All data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

# 4.2 Data Retention Period

  • Configuration Data: Retained while your bot remains active and for a reasonable period after removal
  • Application Data: Retained indefinitely unless you request deletion
  • Operational Logs: Retained indefinitely unless you request deletion
  • Account Data (White-Label): Retained indefinitely unless you request deletion
  • Bot Tokens (White-Label): Retained while your subscription is active and deleted within 30 days of service termination

# 4.3 Data Deletion Requests

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at support@easy-apps.xyz. We will process deletion requests within 30 days. Please note:

  • Deletion may affect bot functionality
  • Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes
  • Operational logs necessary for system integrity may be retained in anonymized form

# 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

# 5.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Service, including:

  • Discord: We interact with Discord's API to provide bot functionality. Discord's collection and use of data is governed by Discord's Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: For White-Label payment processing (see Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Hosting providers: For secure server infrastructure

These third parties are contractually obligated to protect your information and may only use it to provide services to us.

# 5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal processes (court orders, subpoenas, search warrants)
  • Requests from government authorities
  • Legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Protection of our rights, property, or safety, or that of others

# 5.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the successor entity. You will be notified of any such change via email (for White-Label customers) or through the Service.

# 5.4 What We Do Not Do

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Rent your data to advertisers or data brokers
  • Share your data for third-party marketing purposes
  • Disclose your information except as described in this Privacy Policy

# 6. Discord's Role and Responsibilities

# 6.1 Discord Platform Data

Our bots operate on the Discord platform and are subject to Discord's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Discord independently collects and processes data about your Discord usage, including:

  • Messages sent in Discord servers
  • User profiles and activities
  • Server information and membership

We have no control over Discord's data collection practices. Please review Discord's Privacy Policy for information on how Discord handles your data.

# 6.2 Bot Permissions

Our bots request specific Discord permissions to function. These may include:

  • Read Messages/View Channels
  • Send Messages
  • Manage Messages
  • Embed Links
  • Attach Files
  • Read Message History
  • Use Slash Commands
  • Other permissions necessary for bot functionality

You can review and manage bot permissions through Discord's server settings.

# 7. Data Security

# 7.1 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security assessments
  • Monitoring for unauthorized access

# 7.3 Limitations

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You use the Service at your own risk.
